# Keen Keeper

## Description
Keen Keeper is a modern web application designed to help you nurture and maintain your friendships. It allows you to keep track of your interactions with friends, monitor how long it has been since you last connected, and view analytical insights into your communication patterns. Never let a friendship fade away by staying on top of your catch-ups!

## Technologies Used
- **Next.js**
- **React** 
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **daisyUI**
- **Recharts**
- **React Icons**
- **React Toastify**

## 3 Key Features
1. **Friend Status Tracking**: View a comprehensive dashboard of your friends, displaying the days since your last contact and an intuitive urgency status (e.g., overdue, almost due, on-track) to remind you to reach out.
2. **Interaction Logging & Timeline**: Easily record different types of communication events (Texts, Calls, Video Chats) and view a chronologically sorted, filterable timeline of all your past interactions.
3. **Friendship Analytics**: Gain insights into your communication habits with interactive pie charts that visualize the breakdown of your interaction types, powered by Recharts.
